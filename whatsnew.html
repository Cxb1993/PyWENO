What’s new in PyWENO¶

New in v0.5.0:

  • partial domain support for smoothness and reconstruction routines

New in v0.4.0:

  • biasing (ie, upstream and downstream biasing)
  • full domain reconstruction (ie, domain boundaries are not avoided)
